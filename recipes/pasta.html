Pasta

Description

This meaty pasta recipe is delicious and super easy to make. I use a high quality store bought sauce for convenience and even more simplicity. Feel free to change up your protein if you fancy. I prefer ground beef but have had success with grilled chicken and even ground turkey in the past.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 lbs of Ground Beef (80%/20% for the tasty fat) (can be substituted for your choice of protein)
  • 1/2 box of your choice of Pasta (experiment with different kinds for poops and giggles)
  • 1 jar of Pasta Sauce (I only ever use Raos Arrabiata. Trust me, bro)
  • 1 Bell Pepper (Red, Green, Yellow, or Orange. Take your pick)
  • 1 Onion (I like red, but any onion should work just fine)
  • 1-3 cloves of Garlic (add as much or as little as you want; cant go wrong with garlic)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Pepper (to taste)
  • Shredded Cheese (Cheddar, Parmesan, Mozza, Parmegiano Reggiano, etc)(to taste)
